General Eye Checkup

This test helps eye care professionals diagnose potential vision problems, such as nearsightedness (myopia), farsightedness (hyperopia), or astigmatism.

Cataract Services

an operation to remove a cloudy lens (cataract) in your eye and replace it with an artificial one




ICL surgery

a lens implant in your eye goes between your iris and your natural lens.




Cornea

The cornea is the clear, outer part of the eye's focusing system located at the front of the eye



Squit Correction

a surgical procedure that aligns the eyes to improve their coordination and correct misalignment, enhancing visual function and appearance.

Glaucoma Services

Glaucoma services encompass diagnostic tests, medical treatments, and surgical interventions aimed at managing and preventing progression of the eye condition


Orbit & Oculoplasty

Orbit and oculoplasty involve surgical procedures focused on treating eye socket (orbit) and eyelid (oculoplasty) issues, including reconstruction, cosmetic enhancement, and functional improvement.

ROP Screening

ROP screening (Retinopathy of Prematurity screening) is a medical procedure used to detect and monitor abnormal blood vessel growth in the retinas of premature infants.

Medical Retina

Medical retina focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases affecting the retina, a crucial layer at the back of the eye responsible for transmitting visual information to the brain.

Contact lens & Low vision Aids

A contact lens is a thin, curved, and usually transparent disc that is placed directly onto the surface of the eye to correct vision or for cosmetic purposes.

Dry Eye Management & Computer vision syndrome

Pediatric Eye Checkup

DCT/DCR Surgery
